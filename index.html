THE BEATLES

About

The Beatles were an English rock band formed in Liverpool in 1960. The group whose best known line-up comprises John Lenon, Paul Mc Cartney, George Harrison and Ringo Starr are regarded as the most influential band of all time. They were intergral to the development of 1960s popular music's recognition as an form of art.
